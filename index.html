The Digital Realm

As artists in 2018, we have to learn how to adapt to new technologies around us and if we're not careful we can very quickly become outdated. The digital realm is fascinating as it opens many doors and the opportunities are unlimited, you can now explore the traditional forms of art making in a whole new light, so rather than shying away from these changes, I think it's exciting to embrace them.
